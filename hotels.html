Our Partner Hotels

Enjoy your stay in Kenya’s finest hotels — comfort, class, and hospitality that perfectly complement your adventure.

Stay with the Best

We’ve handpicked luxurious and comfortable hotels near each of our key destinations to ensure you have a perfect rest after a day of exploration.

Sarova Imperial Hotel — Kisumu

Located in the heart of Kisumu city, Sarova Imperial offers elegant rooms, rooftop dining, and views of Lake Victoria — ideal for visitors to Impala Park and the lakeside tours.

Rate: From $120 per night (Bed & Breakfast)

Eka Hotel — Eldoret

A modern and classy establishment offering spacious rooms, fine dining, and proximity to Iten — the home of world athletic champions.

Rate: From $95 per night (Half Board)

Lake Nakuru Lodge — Nakuru

Nestled within Lake Nakuru National Park, this lodge gives you a front-row seat to Kenya’s wildlife and flamingos — an authentic safari experience.

Rate: From $110 per night (Full Board)

Thompson Falls Lodge — Nyahururu

Enjoy a calm stay next to the scenic waterfall, with cozy cottages, fresh air, and local cuisine. Perfect for photographers and couples.

Rate: From $80 per night (Bed & Breakfast)

Tea Plantation Resort — Tigoni

Relax in the lush tea fields of Tigoni, just outside Nairobi. A serene countryside escape with modern facilities and garden views.

Rate: From $70 per night (Bed & Breakfast)
